# K388 Pro — Mobile Labeling Printer

## Product Highlights

**4-in-1: Scan + Print + Peel + Label** · **1.5s Full Labeling** · **120mm/s** · **203 DPI** · **5.5" Display**

## Key Features

### Ultra-Compact 4-in-1 Design
Integrated scanning, printing, peeling, and labeling in a single device. Complete the full labeling process in just 1.5 seconds — unmatched speed and precision.

### Professional Scanning
Built-in 1D/2D barcode scanner for instant label verification and asset tracking.

### High-Speed Printing
Up to 120 mm/s print speed at 203 DPI (8 dots/mm) resolution for crisp, clear labels.

### Heavy-Duty Construction
Metal frame design for industrial durability. IP54 sealed with 1.5 m drop resistance.

### Large Display & Smart Power
5.5-inch touch display for easy operation. 10.8V 3000 mAh battery with hot-swap support.
