# DT50P Lite — Lightweight UHF RFID Handheld Reader

## Product Highlights

**Impinj E710 + Gen2X** · **1300+ tags/sec** · **>15m RFID** · **498g Lightweight** · **Dual RFID (HF+UHF)** · **6500mAh**

## Key Features

### Lightweight Design, Perfect Comfort
At just 498 g, the ergonomic pistol-grip design ensures effortless handling for extended usage without compromising comfort.

### Unmatched RFID Performance
Powered by the Impinj E710 chip with Gen2X support, delivering 1,300+ tags/sec read rate and over 15 meters of range — significantly boosting work efficiency.

### Dual RFID — HF + UHF
Combines HF RFID (ISO15693, ISO14443 A/B, Mifare, Felica) for close-range NFC applications with high-power UHF RFID for long-range asset tracking — all in one device.

### Powerful Performance
Qualcomm Octa-core 2.45 GHz processor with Android 13.0 ensures fast response and seamless multitasking for demanding applications.

### Fast & Stable Connectivity
Wi-Fi 6 Ready with 2.4G/5G dual-band, plus 4G cellular for reliable data transmission indoors and outdoors.

### All-Day Endurance
6500 mAh removable battery provides reliable power for full-shift uninterrupted productivity.
