# CT48 — Enterprise Mobile Computer

## Product Highlights

**IP67 Sealed** · **5000mAh Smart Battery** · **BT 5.0** · **Professional Scan Engine**

## Key Features

### High Performance
Equipped with an octa-core 2.2 GHz processor and powered by Android 12.0, delivering fast and responsive operation for demanding enterprise applications.

### Professional Scan Engine
Built-in professional scan engine reads 1D/2D/DPM codes with ease. The specially designed 25° scanning angle allows operators to complete scanning tasks efficiently.

### Comfortable and Flexible Operation
Measuring just 66 mm in width and 252 g in weight. The adjusted key height and large keypad prevent accidental touches during intensive use.

### Fast Autofocus
The rear 13 MP camera ensures fast autofocus, capturing clear images for proof-of-delivery and inspection workflows.

### Loud and Clear Speaker
High-performance 1.5 W speaker delivers 100 dB+ volume for clear audio feedback even in noisy environments. Dual-microphone with noise cancellation.
