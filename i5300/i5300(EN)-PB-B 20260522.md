# i5300 — Compact Smart POS Terminal

## Product Highlights

**Android 13** · **Quad-core 2.0GHz** · **Physical Keypad** · **Smart POS Payment** · **58mm Thermal Printer**

## Key Features

### Ergonomic PIN Entry
3.5-inch responsive touch screen with robust physical keypad for seamless PIN entry and reliable operation.

### Best Value Performance
Android 13 with Quad-core 2.0 GHz processor — optimized for cost-effective, high-performance POS operations.

### Payment & Printing
Supports magnetic stripe, IC, and contactless card payments with integrated 58 mm thermal printing.

### Camera Decoding
Camera decoding supports 1D/2D barcodes for checkout and inventory workflows.
