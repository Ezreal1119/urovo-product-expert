# RFID Capabilities Across Product Lines

UROVO's RFID implementations span HF/NFC (13.56 MHz) and UHF (840–960 MHz) across multiple form factors.

## RFID Technology Map

| Technology | Frequency | Range | Products |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| UHF Long-Range | 840–960 MHz | 15–20 m | DT50P (20m), DT50P Lite (15m), RFG91 (15m+), FR7000 (7–12m) |
| UHF Mid-Range | 840–960 MHz | 1.5 m | DT50D, DT610, DT610 Pro |
| UHF Desktop | 840–960 MHz | <50 cm | FR2000 |
| UHF Printing | 840–960 MHz | — | D81R Series |
| UHF Fixed Box | 840–960 MHz | Ext. antenna | FR1000 |
| HF/NFC Standard | 13.56 MHz | 0–6 cm | CT48/48C, CT58 Series, DT40, DT50, SR5750, U2S, i9000S–i9600 |
| Dual HF+UHF | Both | Both | DT50P Lite |
| No RFID | — | — | CT58S, i5300/L, K-series scanners |
