# CT Series Comparison

The CT series are compact keypad handheld terminals available in 4" and 5.5" display sizes.

## CT48 Series (4" Keypad)

| Feature | [[spec/CT48|CT48]] | [[spec/CT48C|CT48C]] |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Processor | Octa-core 2.2 GHz | Octa-core 2.0 GHz |
| OS | Android 12 | Android 12 |
| Display | 4.0" 480×800 | 4.0" 480×800 |
| Weight | 252 g | 268 g |
| Battery | 5000 mAh | 5000 mAh |
| Camera | 13 MP Rear, 2 MP Front opt | 13 MP Rear, 2 MP Front opt |
| Audio | Standard speaker | 2W speaker (100 dB+) |
| IP Rating | IP67 | IP67 |
| AnTuTu | — | 250,000 (80% faster) |
| Key Diff | 2.2 GHz, lighter | 2W audio, faster Antutu |

**Decision**: CT48 for raw CPU speed; CT48C for loud environments requiring clear audio feedback.

## CT58 Series (5.5" Full-Touch)

| Feature | [[spec/CT58|CT58]] | [[spec/CT58C|CT58C]] | [[spec/CT58S|CT58S]] |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Processor | Octa-core 2.0 GHz | Octa-core 2.0 GHz | Octa-core 2.2 GHz |
| OS | Android 12 | Android 12 / 14 | Android 12 |
| Display | 5.5" 720×1440 | 5.5" 720×1440 | 5.5" 720×1440 |
| Weight | 265 g | 266 g | 266 g |
| IP Rating | IP65 | IP67 | IP67 |
| SIM | Single + eSIM | Dual SIM | Single + eSIM |
| Camera Front | 5 MP | 5 MP | 5 MP |
| Audio | 1.5W speaker | 2W speaker | 1.5W speaker |
| Security | Standard | WPA3 | Standard |
| Operating Temp | -10°C to 50°C | -20°C to 60°C | -20°C to 60°C |
| Key Diff | Budget IP65 | Dual SIM, IP67, WPA3, 2W | 2.2 GHz, IP67, global bands |

**Decision**: CT58 for budget; CT58C for harsh environments & dual SIM; CT58S for single SIM + 2.2 GHz speed.
