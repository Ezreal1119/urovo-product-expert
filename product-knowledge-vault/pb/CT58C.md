# CT58C — Enterprise Mobile Computer

> **Category**: [[categories/handheld-terminal|Handheld Terminal]] | **Specs**: [[spec/CT58C|CT58C Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**IP67 Sealed** · **5000mAh Removable Battery** · **5.5-inch Display** · **Android 12/14** · **2W Speaker**

## Key Features

### 5.5-inch Display
High-brightness 5.5-inch display with fast refresh rate for clear visibility.

### Professional Scan Engine
Built-in scan engine reads 1D/2D, stained, distorted, and damaged barcodes with ease.

### High Performance
Powered by an octa-core 2.0 GHz processor running Android 12 or 14, delivering responsive enterprise-grade performance.

### High-Capacity Battery
Removable 5000 mAh battery with optional charging cradle that supports simultaneous device and battery charging, ensuring all-day operation.

### Industrial-Grade Protection
IP67 sealing and 1.5-meter drop resistance for reliable operation in demanding environments.

## Key Differentiator

The CT58C is the most capable CT58 variant: dual SIM, IP67 (vs CT58's IP65), WPA3 security, 2W speaker, and Android 14 upgrade path — making it suitable for harsh environments and multi-carrier deployments.
