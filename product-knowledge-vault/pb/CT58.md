## Key Features

### High Performance
Powered by an octa-core 2.0 GHz processor running Android 12.0 for responsive enterprise-grade operation.

### Comfortable Operation
Ergonomic design with a thin-bezel form factor for comfortable one-handed use and an immersive viewing experience.

### Professional Scan Engine
Built-in scan engine reads 1D/2D, stained, distorted, and damaged barcodes. The specially designed 25° scanning angle allows operators to complete scanning tasks with ease.

### 5.5-inch Display
High-brightness 5.5-inch display (720 × 1440) with fast refresh rate for clear visibility in varied lighting conditions.

### High-Capacity Battery
Removable 5000 mAh battery ensures all-day operation for full-shift productivity.

### Fast Focusing
Rear 13 MP camera with PDAF fast-focusing technology for quick and clear image capture.
