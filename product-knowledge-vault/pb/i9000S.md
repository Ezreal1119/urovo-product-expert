# i9000S — Rugged Smart POS Payment Terminal

> **Category**: [[categories/pos-terminal|POS Terminal]] | **Specs**: [[spec/i9000S|i9000S Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**Octa-core 2.0 GHz** · **Android 13** · **5" Display** · **1.5m Drop** · **PCI & EMV Certified**

## Key Features

### Powerful Performance
Octa-core 2.0 GHz processor with Android 13 for fast, reliable payment processing.

### Complete Payment Acceptance
Magnetic stripe, IC chip, and contactless NFC payments. PCI and EMV Level 1&2 certified. Supports PayWave, PayPass, American Express Expresspay.

### Integrated Thermal Printer
58 mm receipt printer at 203 DPI, 50–70 mm/s. 30 mm media roll.

### Rugged & Reliable
Built to withstand 1.5 m drops. Compact form factor (184 × 81 × 51 mm, 521 g).

### 5-Inch Display
Clear 720 × 1280 display with customizable function keys for efficient operation.
