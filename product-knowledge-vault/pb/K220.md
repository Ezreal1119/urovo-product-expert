# K220 — Wireless Barcode Scanner with Base

> **Category**: [[categories/barcode-scanner|Barcode Scanner]] | **Specs**: [[spec/K220|K220 Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**Wireless + Base Station** · **1280×1080 CMOS** · **60fps** · **1D/2D Decoding**

## Key Features

### Cordless Freedom
238 g handheld scanner with 216 g charging base. Reliable wireless connectivity for mobile workflows.

### High-Resolution Imaging
1280 × 1080 CMOS sensor at 60 fps for fast, accurate barcode capture.

### Full Decode Capability
All major 1D and 2D symbologies including QR, DataMatrix, PDF417, and postal codes.
