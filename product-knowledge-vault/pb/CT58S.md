# CT58S — Enterprise Mobile Computer

> **Category**: [[categories/handheld-terminal|Handheld Terminal]] | **Specs**: [[spec/CT58S|CT58S Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**IP67 Sealed** · **5000mAh Removable Battery** · **5.5-inch Display** · **2.2 GHz Octa-core** · **Professional Scan Engine**

## Key Features

### 5.5-inch Display
High-brightness 5.5-inch display with fast refresh rate for clear visibility in varied working conditions.

### Professional Scan Engine
Built-in scan engine reads 1D/2D, stained, distorted, and damaged barcodes with ease.

### High Performance
Powered by an octa-core 2.2 GHz processor running Android 12.0, delivering fast and responsive enterprise-grade performance.

### High-Capacity Battery
Removable 5000 mAh battery with optional charging cradle that supports simultaneous device and battery charging for all-day operation.

### Industrial-Grade Protection
IP67 sealing and 1.5-meter drop resistance for reliable operation in demanding environments.

## Key Differentiator

The CT58S combines CT58C's IP67 durability with the fastest CPU (2.2 GHz) in the CT58 series, in a single-SIM form factor with global frequency band support.
