## Key Features

### Built for Extreme Conditions
IP68 rated. 1.8 m drops across full operating temperature range (-20°C to 60°C) without a case. 1000 consecutive 1.0 m tumbles. Raised-edge "Higher Wall Protection" design.

### Breakthrough 50MP Camera
50 MP rear with PDAF captures detailed images in low light. Optional 3D sensing for volume measurement. ARCore certified.

### Powerful Stereo Audio
Front-facing stereo speakers delivering >100 dB with advanced noise reduction for crystal-clear PTT.
