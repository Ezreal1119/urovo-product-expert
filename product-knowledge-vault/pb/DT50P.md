# DT50P — UHF RFID Handheld Reader

> **Category**: [[categories/handheld-terminal|Handheld Terminal]] | **Specs**: [[spec/DT50P|DT50P Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**Impinj E710** · **1300+ tags/sec** · **>20m RFID Range** · **9000mAh Battery** · **IP67 Sealed** · **5.7" Display**

## Key Features

### Superior RFID Performance
Powered by the Impinj E710 chipset for industry-leading RFID reading speed and accuracy. Read rate exceeds 1,300 tags per second.

### Long-Range Reading
High-power RFID antenna with 5 dBi gain achieves read ranges over 20 meters, enabling efficient warehouse and asset management at distance.

### Self Jammer Cancellation (SJC)
Advanced SJC and high-density recognition technology ensure reliable tag reading even in challenging RF environments and crowded tag populations.

### Ergonomic Pistol-Grip Design
Purpose-built form factor reduces operator fatigue and improves scanning efficiency during extended use.

### Thermal Management
Advanced thermal materials with optimized heat dissipation algorithm enable sustained long-duration RFID reading sessions.

### Professional Barcode Scanning
Equipped with a 1D/2D scan engine that handles general, distorted, screen-displayed, and colored barcodes.
