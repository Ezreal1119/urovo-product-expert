# DT630 — Enterprise-Grade Smartphone Terminal

> **Category**: [[categories/enterprise-smartphone|Enterprise Smartphone]] | **Specs**: [[spec/DT630|DT630 Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**10.4mm Ultra-Slim · 236g** · **4nm NPU** · **6.6" FHD+** · **IP68** · **5G + Wi-Fi 6E** · **50MP AI Camera** · **AnTuTu 950K+**

## Key Features

### Ultra-Light & Slim
236 g at just 10.4 mm thin — 19.4% thinner than predecessor. Consumer smartphone aesthetics with enterprise-grade ruggedness.

### 4nm Edge AI
Next-generation 4 nm octa-core processor with dedicated NPU. Android 15, upgradable to Android 20. AnTuTu 950K+.

### 6.6" FHD+ Immersive Display
1080 × 2408 at 500 nits. Clear visibility outdoors for field inspection and logistics work.

### 50 MP AI Camera
True-to-life images and 4K video in low light. AI-powered enhancement turns every shot into actionable insight.

### Innovative Rear Scan Engine
Rear-positioned scanner with line-of-sight design — faster, more natural data capture with less operator fatigue.

### Optional UHF RFID
Impinj Gen2X: 50+ tags/sec at up to 1.2 m for fast, accurate inventory management.

### Industrial Protection
IP68 dust/water resistance, 1.5 m drop, wide temperature range.

## Key Differentiator

The thinnest enterprise device in the portfolio (10.4 mm) with the largest display (6.6"), 4nm NPU for Edge AI, and IP68 protection.
