# K180 — Wireless Barcode Scanner

> **Category**: [[categories/barcode-scanner|Barcode Scanner]] | **Specs**: [[spec/K180|K180 Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**Bluetooth 5.2** · **Millisecond Decoding** · **95g Lightweight** · **Triple Feedback** · **2500mAh**

## Key Features

### Precision Scanning
Professional scan engine for millisecond decoding. Reads 1D/2D including damaged, distorted, laminated, screen-displayed, and low-contrast barcodes.

### Lightweight & Ergonomic
Whale-shaped curved design at just 95 g. Easy grip, smooth handling, and comfort during extended use.

### Seamless Connectivity
Bluetooth 5.2 pairing with smartphones, tablets, notebooks, and desktops.

### Triple Scan Feedback
Vibration motor, buzzer, and LED indicator provide real-time feedback.
