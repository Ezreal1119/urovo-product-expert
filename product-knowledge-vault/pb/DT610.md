# DT610 — Lightweight RFID Mobile Computer

> **Category**: [[categories/enterprise-smartphone|Enterprise Smartphone]] | **Specs**: [[spec/DT610|DT610 Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**222g · 12.9mm** · **Qualcomm Q-6690** · **5G + Wi-Fi 7** · **BT 6.0** · **UHF RFID** · **AnTuTu 1.1M** · **On-Screen NFC**

## Key Features

### Lightest 5G UHF RFID Device
At 222 g and under 13 mm thin, the DT610 is the lightest enterprise device with integrated UHF RFID and 5G — smartphone ergonomics without compromise.

### Redefined Performance
Qualcomm Q-6690: octa-core up to 2.9 GHz, 6 TOPS AI, Android 15 (upgradable to Android 19). AnTuTu 1,114,080.

### Next-Gen Wireless
5G + Wi-Fi 7 (2×2 MU-MIMO, 5,764.7 Mbps) with 4× lower latency and 5× greater capacity vs Wi-Fi 6. Bluetooth 6.0.

### Integrated UHF RFID
Impinj Gen2X: 50+ tags/sec at up to 1.5 m. Purpose-built for warehouse inventory, retail stocktaking, and asset tracking.

### 6" FHD+ Sunlight-Readable Display
1080 × 2160, Corning Gorilla Glass. Clear visibility even in truck cabs and direct sun.

### FlexRange Scanning
5 cm to 10 m working range — read barcodes without bending, stretching, or climbing.

### On-Screen NFC
Tap-to-pay and data exchange directly through the display. EMVCo PCD L1 compliant.

### AI Smart Battery
4000 mAh (4950 mAh optional), AI-driven charging, hot-swap for continuous multi-shift operation.

## Key Differentiator

The only enterprise device combining Wi-Fi 7, Bluetooth 6.0, 5G, and UHF RFID in a 222 g, 12.9 mm form factor.
