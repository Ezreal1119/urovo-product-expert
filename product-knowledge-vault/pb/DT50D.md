# DT50D — Portable UHF RFID Reader

> **Category**: [[categories/handheld-terminal|Handheld Terminal]] | **Specs**: [[spec/DT50D|DT50D Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**UHF RFID** · **IP67 Sealed** · **5.7" Touch Screen** · **Octa-core 2.45 GHz** · **1.5m / 3m RFID Range**

## Key Features

### Integrated UHF RFID
High-performance UHF antenna design captures multiple tags instantly. RFID module seamlessly integrated into an elegant, phone-like form factor.

### Professional Barcode Scanning
Embedded leading 1D/2D scan engine adapts to poorly printed, distorted, and damaged barcodes.

### Wide Application
Ideal for retail shops, jewelry houses, bank vaults, ticket gates, hospitals, and more.

### Premium Performance
Octa-core 2.45 GHz processor with Android 11/13 delivers responsive enterprise-grade operation.

### Industrial Durability
IP67 sealing and 1.5-meter drop resistance for reliable use in demanding environments.

## Key Differentiator

The DT50D is the only handheld form factor in the DT50 lineup with built-in UHF RFID — no pistol grip or external sled needed.
