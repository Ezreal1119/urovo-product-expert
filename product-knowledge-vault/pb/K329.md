# K329 — Mobile Printer

> **Category**: [[categories/mobile-printer|Mobile Printer]] | **Specs**: [[spec/K329|K329 Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**120mm/s Print Speed** · **BT 4.2/5.0 + NFC** · **2-3 inch** · **Receipts & Labels** · **Two-Color Printing**

## Key Features

### Dual Capabilities: Receipts & Labels
Supports both receipt and label printing for versatile deployment across retail, transportation, and manufacturing.

### Quick Pairing
Bluetooth 4.2 LE and passive NFC tags for instant pairing with mobile devices.

### Long-Lasting Endurance
Intelligent power consumption control for extended work shifts. 15-day standby.

### Multi-Language Compatible
TSPL, CPCL, ESC/POS, ZPL, JPL support. Visual editing app "YinYin" with express delivery face sheet integration.
