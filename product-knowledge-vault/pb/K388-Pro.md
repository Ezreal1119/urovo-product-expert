# K388 Pro — Mobile Labeling Printer

> **Category**: [[categories/mobile-printer|Mobile Printer]] | **Specs**: [[spec/K388-Pro|K388 Pro Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**4-in-1: Scan+Print+Peel+Label** · **1.5s Full Process** · **120mm/s** · **Android 14** · **5.5" Display**

## Key Features

### Ultra-Compact 4-in-1 Design
Integrated scanning, printing, peeling, and labeling. Complete labeling in 1.5 seconds.

### Professional Scanning
Built-in 1D/2D barcode scanner for instant label verification.

### High-Speed Printing
Up to 120 mm/s at 203 DPI. ≤58 mm media width.

### Heavy-Duty Construction
Metal frame, 1.5 m drop. 10.8V 3000mAh battery with hot-swap.

## Key Differentiator
Only product combining scan+print+peel+label in one device with full Android 14 computing.
