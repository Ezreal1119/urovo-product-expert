# SR5750 — Android Wearable Computer

> **Category**: [[categories/barcode-scanner|Barcode Scanner]] | **Specs**: [[spec/SR5750|SR5750 Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**Android 13 · 2.06" Display · 70g · IP67 · 1.8m Drop · Wi-Fi 6 · 1150mAh**

## Key Features

### Full Android on Your Wrist
Octa-core 2.0 GHz + Android 13. 2.1" AMOLED touchscreen with Watch UI. Complete workflows directly on the device.

### Pro Scanning + 13MP Camera
Millisecond barcode decoding and 13 MP PDAF images simultaneously. In-display NFC for login and data exchange.

### Industrial Wearable
70 g. 4 wearing modes (wrist/finger/hand/neck). 4 physical buttons. IP67 + 1.8 m drop. 1150 mAh rechargeable battery.
