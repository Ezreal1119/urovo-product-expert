# i9600 — Flagship Smart POS Payment Terminal

> **Category**: [[categories/pos-terminal|POS Terminal]] | **Specs**: [[spec/i9600|i9600 Specification]]

## Product Highlights

**Dual Screens (6.745" + 2.5")** · **80mm 150mm/s Printer** · **Octa-core Option** · **PCI & EMV**

## Key Features

### Expansive Dual-Screen Design
6.745-inch HD main display paired with a 2.5-inch color touch customer display for clear, efficient interaction.

### High-Speed 80mm Printer
Fast 150 mm/s thermal printing with 80 mm media width — wider format, faster receipts than all other POS models.

### Flagship Performance
Upgraded 64-bit Android 13 system. Optional Octa-core 2.0 GHz processor for the most responsive POS experience.

### All-in-One Payment
Integrated magnetic stripe, IC chip, and contactless payment module. PCI PTS 6.x and EMV Level 1 & 2 certified.

## Key Differentiator

Only POS in the portfolio with dual screens and 80mm high-speed (150mm/s) printer.
