# FR7000 Series — Fixed UHF RFID Reader

## Product Highlights

**Impinj E710** · **1300+ tags/sec** · **99.7% Accuracy** · **7m / 12m Range** · **6dBi or 9dBi Antenna**

## Key Features

### Two Antenna Options
- **6 dBi** (130×130×50mm, 600g): >7 m range
- **9 dBi** (257×257×52mm, 1800g): >12 m range

### Superior Performance
1300+ tags/sec with 99.7% accuracy. Adjustable 0–33 dBm output.

### Industrial Durability
Plastic + aluminum alloy casing. Versatile interfaces: RS232, RJ45, RS485/Wiegand/GPIO/USB optional.
