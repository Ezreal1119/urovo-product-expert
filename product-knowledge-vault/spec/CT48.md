## Overview

The CT48 is a compact, keypad-equipped handheld terminal designed for data-intensive enterprise workflows. With IP67 sealing, a professional 2D scan engine, and glove/wet-finger touch support, it is purpose-built for logistics, warehousing, and retail environments.

## Key Differentiators

- **Professional 2D Imager**: Full 1D/2D/Postal barcode support including screen-displayed codes
- **Compact Keypad Form Factor**: Physical numeric keypad + function keys for rapid data entry
- **Glove & Wet-Finger Touch**: Ultra-sensitive capacitive touch panel usable in harsh conditions
- **NFC Optional**: HF RFID for contactless applications when needed

## Target Industries

Logistics, Warehousing, Retail, Field Service
