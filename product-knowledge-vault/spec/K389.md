# K389 — Mobile Printer

> Source: K389 Product Spec Sheet.md

Category: [[categories/mobile-printer|Mobile Printer]]


## Overview

Built for demanding industrial and mobile environments, the K389 3-inch portable printer features an exclusive rugged casing that withstands 2-meter drops onto concrete. Designed for enterprise-grade workflows across logistics, field service, and industrial applications.

## Product Highlights

**2m Drop Resistance** — Exclusive protective casing withstands 2-meter drops from any angle onto concrete. Built for the toughest manufacturing and warehouse environments.

**Compact & Portable** — Weighing only 390 g. Carry effortlessly throughout your shift to print labels where and when needed — eliminating movement between picking locations and printers.

**Double the Shift, Single Charge** — High-capacity 3200 mAh battery with intelligent power management delivers up to double the runtime of previous models.

**Seamless Connectivity** — Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) dual-band (2.4G/5G), Bluetooth 5.2, USB-C. Robust, reliable wireless communication for seamless system integration.

**High-Speed Precision Printing** — Fast, accurate label printing for mobile workforce efficiency. Compatible with multiple programming languages.
