## Overview

The SR5750 is a fully integrated, enterprise-grade Android wearable computer built for warehouse operations. At just 70 g, it combines a 2.1-inch AMOLED touchscreen, professional scanning, and full Android 13 computing — eliminating the need for a separate mobile computer and ring scanner.

## Product Highlights

**Full Android Computer on Your Wrist** — Octa-core 2.0 GHz, Android 13, 2.1" AMOLED touchscreen. Complete end-to-end workflows directly on the device.

**2.06" Touch Display** — Compact 410 × 502 touchscreen for wrist-worn workflows.

**Wi-Fi 6 + BT 5.3** — Seamless real-time data flow with high-speed, low-latency connectivity. Dense device support with enhanced security and reduced congestion.

**Professional Scanning + 13MP Camera** — Millisecond barcode decoding and high-resolution image capture simultaneously.

**In-Display NFC** — Instant login, seamless data exchange, and quick accessory changes without interrupting workflows.

**Flexible Wearing** — Wrist, finger, back of hand, or neck strap. Four physical buttons for true hands-free operation.

**Battery & Durability** — 1150 mAh rechargeable battery, IP67 sealing, and 1.8 m drop resistance.
