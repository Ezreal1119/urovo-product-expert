# RFG91 — Rfid Sled

> Source: RFG91 Product Spec Sheet.md

Category: [[categories/rfid-sled|Rfid Sled]]


## Overview

The RFG91 transforms smartphones and mobile computers into professional-grade UHF RFID readers. Powered by Impinj E710 with Gen2X support, it delivers 1300+ tags/sec at 15 m+ range. At 390 g with 4 flexible form factors, it provides multi-platform compatibility across Android, iOS, and Windows.

## Product Highlights

**Impinj E710 + Gen2X** — 1300+ tags/sec, 15 m+ read range. Professional-grade RFID performance in a sled form factor.

**4 Form Factors** — Standard, Magnetic, POGO PIN, and Universal versions for flexible deployment with any host device.

**Multi-Platform** — Android, iOS, Windows compatibility. NFC, Bluetooth, and QR code pairing for fast connection.

**All-in-One Data Collection** — RFID reading + barcode scanning in one device.

**Flexible Battery Options** — 4900 mAh standard battery with optional 6700 mAh high-capacity battery for extended operation.
