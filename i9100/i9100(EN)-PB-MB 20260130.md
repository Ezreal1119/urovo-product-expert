# i9100 — Smart POS Payment Terminal

## Product Highlights

**Android 13** · **Quad-core 2.0 GHz** · **5.5" Display** · **PCI & EMV Certified** · **All-in-One Payment**

## Key Features

### All-in-One Payment
Accepts all payment methods: Magnetic Card, IC Card, Contactless Card, and QR Code Payment. Complete payment certifications with fully guaranteed transaction security.

### Excellent Performance
Powered by Android 13 and Quad-core 2.0 GHz high-speed processor for fast, reliable payment processing.

### PCI & EMV Certified
PCI PTS 6.x and EMV Level 1 & 2 certified for maximum payment security.
