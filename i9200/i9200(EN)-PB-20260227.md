# i9200 — Smart POS Payment Terminal

## Product Highlights

**6.5" Large Display** · **Quad/Octa-core** · **Android 13** · **PCI & EMV Certified** · **58mm Thermal Printer**

## Key Features

### Large 6.5-Inch Display
720 × 1600 high-definition display for clear, efficient operation.

### Flexible Performance
Quad-core or Octa-core A53 2.0 GHz processor options. 2 GB + 16 GB or 3 GB + 32 GB memory configurations.

### Complete Payment Acceptance
Magnetic stripe, IC chip, and contactless NFC payments. PCI PTS 6.x and EMV certified.

### Integrated Thermal Printer
58 mm receipt printer at 203 DPI for fast transaction processing.
