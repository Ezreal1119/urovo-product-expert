# FR7000 Series — Fixed UHF RFID Reader

## Product Highlights

**Impinj E710** · **1300+ tags/sec** · **99.7% Accuracy** · **7m / 12m Range** · **6dBi or 9dBi Antenna**

## Key Features

### Superior UHF RFID Performance
Powered by Impinj E710 chip, achieving 1300+ tags/sec reading speed with 99.7% accuracy — reliably capturing swiftly moving items.

### Two Antenna Options
Choose 6 dBi (up to 7 m) or 9 dBi (up to 12 m) circularly polarized antenna with adjustable output power. Suitable for warehouse management, logistics sorting, vehicle access control, and archives & library management.

### Industrial-Grade Durability
Robust plastic and aluminum alloy casing with industrial-grade dust and water protection — resilient against dust, oil, and moisture.

### Versatile Interfaces
RS232, RJ45 standard; optional RS485, Wiegand, GPIO, USB 2.0 for flexible system integration.

### Integrated Antenna Design
All-in-one design for easy deployment without external antenna cabling complexity.
