# DT630 — Enterprise-Grade Smartphone Terminal

## Overview

Breaking from traditional bulky PDA designs, the DT630 is an ultra-slim (10.4 mm, 236 g) enterprise smartphone with IP68 protection. Powered by a 4 nm octa-core processor with dedicated NPU for Edge AI, it delivers premium portability, 5G + Wi-Fi 6E connectivity, and a 6.6-inch FHD+ display.

**AnTuTu score: 950,000+**

## Product Highlights

**Ultra-Light & Slim** — 236 g, 10.4 mm thin (19.4% thinner than predecessor). Smartphone aesthetics with enterprise-grade performance.

**Innovative Rear Scan Engine** — Rear-positioned scanner with intuitive line-of-sight scanning design. Enables faster, more natural data capture while reducing operator fatigue during long shifts.

**Superior Performance** — 4 nm octa-core with NPU, Android 15 (upgradable to Android 20), AnTuTu 950K+. Ready for Edge AI applications.

**50 MP Camera + AI** — True-to-life images and 4K video even in low light. AI-powered enhancement turns every shot into actionable insight.

**6.6" FHD+ Display** — 1080 × 2408, 500 nits brightness for clear outdoor visibility. Ideal for field inspection, logistics, and outdoor work.

**Next-Gen Wireless** — 5G + Wi-Fi 6E seamless low-latency connectivity for real-time data flow throughout operations.

**Optional UHF RFID** — Impinj Gen2X: 50+ tags/sec at up to 1.2 m for fast, accurate inventory management.

**AI Smart Battery** — 4500 mAh with AI-driven intelligent charging that optimizes efficiency and extends battery lifespan.

**Biometric Security** — Fingerprint sensor integrated into power button.

**Industrial Protection** — IP68 dust/water resistance, 1.5 m drop resistance, wide temperature range.
