# U2S — Wearable Computer

## Product Highlights

**Hands-Free Wearable** · **Octa-core 2.4GHz** · **Android 14 GMS** · **Dual Scanners** · **Hot-Swap Battery** · **IP65**

## Key Features

### Hands-Free Design
Wearable form factor with hands-free mobility. Lighter and more compact for all-day comfort.

### Smooth Performance
Qualcomm Octa-core 2.4 GHz processor with Android 14 GMS for smooth, efficient task execution.

### Dual Scan Engines
Standard + Long Range scan engines for versatile barcode capture across different distances.

### Hot-Swap Power
3360 mAh battery with hot-swap and low-temperature support. Optional battery for extended temperature range (-30°C to 60°C).
