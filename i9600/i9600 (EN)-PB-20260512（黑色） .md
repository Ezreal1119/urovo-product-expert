# i9600 — Smart POS Payment Terminal

## Product Highlights

**Dual Screens (6.745" + 2.5")** · **58mm Thermal Printer** · **Octa-core CPU Option** · **PCI & EMV Certified** · **Modular Design**

## Key Features

### Expansive Dual-Screen Design
6.745-inch HD main display paired with a 2.5-inch color touch customer display for clear, efficient interaction.

### Flagship Performance
Upgraded 64-bit Android 13 system. Optional Octa-core 2.0 GHz processor (standard Quad-core 2.0 GHz) for smooth, responsive operation.

### Smart Customer Interaction
Dual-screen design enables better customer engagement during transactions.

### Thermal Printer
Integrated 58 mm thermal printer with 80 mm/s print speed.

### All-in-One Payment
Integrated magnetic stripe, IC chip, and contactless payment module. PCI PTS 6.x and EMV Level 1 & 2 certified.
