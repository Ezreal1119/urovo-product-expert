# DT610 Pro — Ultra-Rugged Mobile Computer

## Overview

The DT610 Pro combines cutting-edge technology with enterprise-grade durability for mission-critical frontline operations. Powered by the Qualcomm Q-6690 with integrated UHF RFID, it delivers edge AI, 5G + Wi-Fi 7, and Bluetooth 6.0 in a sleek yet ultra-rugged form factor with IP68 protection.

## Product Highlights

**Slim Design, Superior Protection** — Enhanced raised-edge protection in a sleek 270 g form factor. IP68 rated for full dust and water protection in heavy rain and harsh industrial environments.

**Extreme Durability** — 1.8 m drops across full operating temperature range (-20°C to 60°C) without a case. 1000 consecutive 1.0 m tumbles.

**Breakthrough Enterprise Processor** — Qualcomm Q-6690 with fully integrated UHF RFID. Octa-core up to 2.9 GHz, 6 INT8 TOPS AI, Android 15.

**Next-Gen Wireless** — 5G + Wi-Fi 7 (2×2 MU-MIMO) + Bluetooth 6.0 + CBRS private network support. 4.8× faster, 4× lower latency, 5× greater capacity.

**Advanced 50 MP Camera** — 50 MP rear with PDAF captures detailed images in low light. ARCore support. Optional 3D sensing for volume measurement.

**Brilliant FHD+ Display** — 6-inch 1080×2160, 600 nits sunlight-readable, Corning Gorilla Glass.

**FlexRange Scanning** — 5 cm to 10 m barcode reading range without bending or stretching.

**AI Smart Battery** — 5500 mAh with AI-powered management and hot-swap for uninterrupted full-shift operation.

**Loud & Clear Audio** — Front-facing stereo speakers delivering >100 dB output with advanced noise reduction for crystal-clear PTT communication.

**Integrated UHF RFID** — Impinj Gen2X: 50+ tags/sec at up to 1.5 m.

**Biometric Security** — Fingerprint sensor integrated into power button.
