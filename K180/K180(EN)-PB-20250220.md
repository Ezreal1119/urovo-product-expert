# K180 — Wireless Barcode Scanner

## Product Highlights

**Bluetooth 5.2** · **Millisecond Decoding** · **95g Lightweight** · **Triple Feedback** · **2500mAh Battery**

## Key Features

### Precision Scanning
Equipped with a professional scan engine for millisecond decoding. Effortlessly reads 1D/2D barcodes including damaged, distorted, laminated, screen-displayed, and low-contrast codes.

### Lightweight & Ergonomic
Whale-shaped curved design with compact form (95 g). Easy grip, smooth handling, and comfort during extended use.

### Seamless Connectivity
Bluetooth 5.2 pairing with smartphones, tablets, notebooks, and desktops for broad compatibility and effortless integration.

### Triple Scan Feedback
Vibration motor, buzzer, and LED indicator provide real-time feedback in any environment, preventing missed scans.
