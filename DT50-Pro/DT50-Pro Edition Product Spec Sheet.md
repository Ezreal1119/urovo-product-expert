# DT50-Pro Edition — Enterprise Mobile Computer

## Overview

Optimized to meet evolving enterprise mobility demands, the DT50-Pro Edition delivers enhanced performance, faster connectivity, and reliable data capture — while maintaining full compatibility with existing accessories.

- **Performance:** Octa-core 2.4 GHz, Android 16 GMS, AnTuTu 418,013 (ANTUTU V11) — 20% faster than predecessor
- **Connectivity:** Wi-Fi 6E (2×2 MU-MIMO) with 1,200 MHz additional clean spectrum; Bluetooth 5.3 with 13% improved connection stability
- **Camera:** 16 MP rear captures 23% more detail; faster focus and clearer low-light imaging
- **Battery:** Up to 50% longer battery life; replaceable 5000 mAh (5200 mAh optional)
- **Durability:** IP67, 1.5 m drop (1.8 m with rugged case), 1000 × 1.0 m tumbles

## Product Highlights

**Enhanced Performance** — 2.4 GHz + Android 16 + AnTuTu 418K for demanding, data-intensive applications.

**Stronger Connectivity** — Wi-Fi 6E leverages 6 GHz band for reduced interference and latency. Wider channels deliver faster, more reliable data transmission in high-density environments.

**Enhanced Bluetooth Stability** — BT 5.3 delivers 13% better connection stability vs. previous generation for consistent peripheral and backend communication.

**Advanced Data Capture** — Professional scan engine + 16 MP rear camera with 23% more detail. Faster focusing and clearer imaging even in low light.

**Long-Lasting Power** — Replaceable 5000 mAh battery with up to 50% longer life. Fast charging support minimizes downtime.

**Large Clear Display** — 5.7-inch full-screen (720 × 1440, 1080 × 2160 optional), 450 nits, improved readability.

**Seamless Ecosystem Compatibility** — Pogo-pin connectors on back and bottom; full compatibility with existing charging cradles, trigger handles, and mounting solutions.

**Optimized Audio** — High-performance speaker for loud, clear output in noisy environments (warehouses, retail, factories).

**Industrial-Grade Durability** — IP67 sealing, 1.5 m drop resistance, wide temperature range.
