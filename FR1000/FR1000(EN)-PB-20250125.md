# FR1000 — Intelligent UHF RFID Fixed Reader

## Product Highlights

**Impinj E710** · **1300+ tags/sec** · **8 SMA Antenna Ports** · **PoE+ Supply** · **HDMI 1080P Output**

## Key Features

### Superior RFID Performance
Powered by Impinj E710 chipset for high-speed, accurate tag reading. Processes quantities of RFID labels rapidly, significantly improving work efficiency.

### 8 External Antenna Ports
Eight SMA antenna interfaces for flexible configuration in complex environments — cover wide areas with ease.

### High-Performance Processing
Octa-core 64-bit 1.6 GHz processor provides fast, secure data processing. Android 10 OS.

### Comprehensive Connectivity
Bluetooth 5.0, 2.4G/5G dual-band Wi-Fi, GPS+GLONASS or GPS+BeiDou positioning. Manual IPv6 address configuration.

### Industrial Design
High thermal material in a discreet chassis. PoE+ power supply option for simplified deployment. HDMI D-Type output up to 1920 × 1200.

### Extreme Environment Ready
Operates from -30°C to 70°C. Storage from -40°C to 85°C.
