# DT50 — Enterprise Mobile Computer

## Product Highlights

**Wi-Fi 6 Ready** · **5000mAh Battery** · **5.7" Full Display** · **2.45 GHz Octa-core** · **Advanced Dual-Lens Scan Engine**

## Key Features

### Premium Performance
Powered by an octa-core 2.45 GHz processor running Android 13, enabling seamless multitasking across demanding enterprise applications.

### Wi-Fi 6 Ready
802.11ax ready for faster transmission speeds, lower latency, and enhanced security in high-density environments.

### Advanced Scan Engine
Dual-lens architecture with fast near-and-far switching mechanism — read range from 6 cm to 10 m for versatile barcode capture.

### Fast & Clear Imaging
13 MP rear camera with PDAF technology captures sharp, clear images within milliseconds.

### Large 5.7-inch Display
Full-screen 5.7-inch display with high sensitivity and ultra-brightness for excellent visibility in any lighting.

### Long-Lasting Endurance
High-capacity 5000 mAh battery provides full-shift power for continuous operations.
