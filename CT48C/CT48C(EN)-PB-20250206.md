# CT48C — Enterprise Mobile Computer

## Product Highlights

**IP67 Sealed** · **5000mAh Battery** · **BT 5.0** · **2W High-Power Speaker** · **Professional Scan Engine**

## Key Features

### High Performance
Powered by an octa-core 2.0 GHz processor running Android 12.0, the CT48C achieves an AnTuTu benchmark score of 250,000 — an 80% improvement over the previous generation — delivering a fast and responsive user experience.

### High-Precision Scanning Engine
Equipped with a million-pixel image sensor that captures and reads various barcode types — 1D, 2D, stained, distorted, and DPM — with lightning-fast response and pinpoint accuracy.

### Zero-Handoff Roaming over Wi-Fi
Enables seamless roaming among access points, ensuring uninterrupted and rapid data transmission for mobile workflows.

### Ergonomic Design with Keypads
Enhanced key spacing allows flexible operation even with gloves. Ergonomic form factor designed for comfortable one-handed use.

### High-Volume 2W Speaker
Optimized 2W power speaker delivers 100 dB+ volume for crystal-clear audio feedback, even in noisy environments.
