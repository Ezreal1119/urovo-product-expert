# DT66 — Premium 5G Rugged Mobile Computer

## Product Highlights

**5G + Wi-Fi 6E** · **6.5" UHD Display** · **IP68** · **Hot-Swap Battery** · **iToF 3D Camera** · **Dual-Side NFC**

## Key Features

### Immersive 6.5-inch Display
Ultra-high-definition (720 × 1600) thin-bezel display shows more information per screen with less scrolling, improving workflow efficiency.

### Dual-Side NFC Reading
Supports both in-display and backside NFC reading to meet diverse usage scenarios.

### 24-Hour Hot-Swap Power
5000 mAh smart battery with built-in battery management system extends overall battery life. Hot-swap capability enables uninterrupted 24-hour operation.

### Qualcomm Performance
Powered by the latest Qualcomm 2.4 GHz high-performance processor, delivering exceptional processing speed for faster 1D/2D/DPM barcode decoding.

### Next-Gen Connectivity
Wi-Fi 6E (2.4G/5G/6G tri-band) and 5G cellular deliver super-fast, reliable wireless connections for always-on operations.

### Optional iToF 3D Camera
iToF camera enables three-dimensional volume measurement of parcels, ideal for logistics and warehouse dimensioning applications.
